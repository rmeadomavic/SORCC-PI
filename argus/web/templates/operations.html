How to view in Google Earth

  1. Click "Download KML File" above
  2. Transfer the file to your computer
  3. Open Google Earth Pro
  4. Drag and drop the .kml file into Google Earth
  5. RF sources will appear as placemarks on the map

Manual export (from terminal)

sudo kismetdb_to_kml -v --in Kismet-*.kismet --out survey.kml
